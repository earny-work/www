Why Earny?

Our solution works without any of the hassles of other platforms. Earnies are paid in cash as soon as the job is done, and employers pay their Earnies from an easy to top up pre-paid balance.
Earny has no age restrictions, young or old. Whether it's your first time working, or you are looking for something to do during retirement, Earny is the right solution for you!
Earny is designed for the next gen economy. We help people find casual work like babysitting, lawnmowing, anything! We then match them with other people who are looking for someone to do these odd jobs.
